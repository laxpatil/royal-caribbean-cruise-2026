Costa Maya, Mexico

Easy, relaxed port · Pier splash area · Stroller-friendly

☀️ ~30°C / 86°F  ·  Sunny & Humid
💰
Estimated Budget
$40 – $70 USD
Beach palapa · Lunch · Drinks · Souvenirs
7:00 – 8:00 AM

🍳 Breakfast on the Ship

Fill up before disembarking. Feed your toddler well and pack snacks, milk & water in a bag — port meal timing can be unpredictable.

🚢 Do This First 🍌 Pack Snacks
8:30 – 9:30 AM

🏖️ Costa Maya Pier Plaza

The pier has a beautiful free pool, splash area, and sandy beach — ideal for toddlers! The Mayan Ruins replica area is shaded and photo-worthy. Let your little one explore the colorful open-air market stalls.

👶 Splash Area 🚶 Easy Stroll 🌴 Shaded 💵 Free
9:30 – 11:30 AM

🌊 Mahahual Beach

A short taxi (~5 min, ~$5–8) to Mahahual — a quaint Caribbean beach village. Water is calm, shallow, and crystal clear — ideal for your 16-month-old to paddle and play. Rent a beachside palapa (~$15–20) and relax.

👶 Shallow Water 🌊 Super Relaxed 🚕 ~$5–8 taxi
11:30 AM – 1:00 PM

🌮 Lunch at Mahahual Beach Bar

Enjoy fresh fish tacos, guacamole & coconut agua fresca at a beach restaurant along the Malecón. Laid-back, toddler-friendly vibes. Great spot for a stroller nap! 😴

🌮 Fish Tacos 😴 Stroller Nap 💵 ~$15–25
1:00 – 2:30 PM

🌴 Back to Pier — Pool & Shopping

Head back to the pier. Let your toddler enjoy the free pier pool while you browse artisan shops. Pick up vanilla extract, hammocks, or Mayan masks as souvenirs.

🏊 Free Pool 🛍️ Souvenir Shopping
2:30 – 3:30 PM

⛵ Return to Ship

Back on board 60–90 min before departure. Rinse off, change your toddler, and hit the pool deck for a pre-dinner dip! 🌅

⚠️ Don't Miss the Ship! 🌅 Pool Deck Sunset

💡 Costa Maya Tips

▼
  • 🌞Apply reef-safe sunscreen every 90 min — the April sun is intense!
  • 🧴Bring a portable shade tent — the pier beach has limited shade.
  • 💵USD is accepted everywhere. Carry small bills ($1–$5) for tips.
  • 🚶The pier is fully stroller-friendly — no excursion needed!
  • 🦺Skip cenotes & ruins tours with a 16-month-old — save for next trip!
  • 🧊Stay hydrated! Bring extra water bottles for your toddler.

Roatán, Honduras

Stunning coral reef island · Lush & tropical · West Bay Beach

🌤️ ~29°C / 85°F  ·  Tropical & Breezy
💰
Estimated Budget
$70 – $110 USD
Taxi · Beach chairs · Lunch · Snorkeling · Souvenirs
7:00 – 8:00 AM

🍳 Breakfast on the Ship

Same strategy — fill up on board. Pack a snack bag for the toddler and pre-apply sunscreen before heading off.

🚢 Start Strong 🥞 Ship Breakfast
8:30 – 10:00 AM

🏝️ West Bay Beach

Consistently rated one of the most beautiful beaches in the Caribbean. Water is crystal-clear and calm — like a natural bathtub for your toddler. A taxi (~$10–15) or water shuttle (~$5–10) from the pier. Arrive early to claim a shaded palapa!

👶 Calm Crystal Water 🏖️ World-Class Beach 🚕 ~$10–15 taxi
10:00 – 11:00 AM

🤿 Take-Turns Snorkeling!

West Bay sits on one of the best coral reefs in the Western Hemisphere. One parent snorkels while the other stays in the shallows with the toddler, then swap! The reef starts just 10–15 meters from shore — no boat needed.

🤿 Take Turns 👶 Shallow Play 🐠 Amazing Marine Life
11:00 AM – 12:30 PM

🥘 Beachfront Lunch

Try Sundowner's or Bite on the Beach — right on the sand. Fresh ceviche, grilled fish, and tropical drinks. Relaxed and family-friendly. Perfect time for a toddler nap under the palapa! 😴

🐟 Fresh Ceviche 😴 Post-Swim Nap 💵 ~$25–40
12:30 – 2:00 PM

🦜 Gumbalimba Park (optional)

If your toddler is energized — Gumbalimba Park has tropical gardens, macaws, monkeys, and a beach all-in-one. Stroller-friendly, shaded paths. Macaws landing on shoulders = magical memory! 📸

🦜 Macaws & Monkeys! 🌿 Shaded Jungle Walk 💵 ~$25–35/adult
2:00 – 3:30 PM

🛍️ Mahogany Bay Pier

Head back to the pier — browse Honduran coffee, wooden carvings & jewelry. One parent could try the pier zipline while the other chills with the toddler!

🛍️ Local Crafts 🤸 Pier Zipline!
3:30 – 4:00 PM

⛵ Board the Ship

Rinse off, settle in, and enjoy a golden Caribbean sunset from the deck with your little one. A perfect end to a magical Roatán day! 🌅

🌅 Sunset on Deck ⚠️ Back Before All-Aboard!

💡 Roatán Tips

▼
  • 🚕Agree on taxi fares before you get in — $10–15 to West Bay is fair.
  • 🤿Bring your own toddler swim vest — rentals for infants are rare.
  • 🌊West Bay has no harsh waves — extremely safe for toddlers.
  • 🦟Pack insect repellent — jungle areas have mosquitoes.
  • 📱Download offline maps — roaming data is expensive in Honduras.
  • 💵USD accepted everywhere — Lempiras rarely needed for tourists.

🏖️ Option A — Chill Island (South Beach)

$0 · Included with cruise

Beautiful free beach with calm, shallow turquoise water — ideal for your toddler. Plenty of lounge chairs & umbrellas (first-come). Less crowded than the main areas. Grab a spot early!

  • ✅ Free lounge chairs
  • ✅ Calm shallow water
  • ✅ Less crowded
  • ✅ Toddler perfect
  • ⚠️ No food service — walk to food trucks
⭐ Our Pick

🌴 Option B — Coco Beach Club

~$75–99/adult · Kids under 3 free

Premium open-air beach club with overwater cabanas, infinity pool, and pristine private beach. Includes lunch buffet, dedicated bar, and towel service. The infinity pool has a shallow kiddie section. Stunning for photos! 📸

  • ✅ Infinity pool + kiddie area
  • ✅ Lunch buffet included
  • ✅ Private calm beach
  • ✅ Server brings drinks to you
  • ✅ Overwater cabanas (extra $$)
  • 📸 Most Instagrammable
💎 Splurge

🛖 Option C — Overwater Cabana

~$350–600/day · Fits up to 8 people

The ultimate private experience — your own overwater bungalow with a slide into the ocean, hammock, private attendant, premium bar, and lunch included. A once-in-a-lifetime family memory. Split cost with another family = very doable!

  • ✅ Private slide into ocean
  • ✅ Personal attendant
  • ✅ All food & drinks included
  • ✅ Nap area for toddler
  • 💡 Split with friends = ~$175/family

🤿 Snorkeling Options — Take Turns!

⭐ Best Value

🐠 Snorkel from South Beach (Free!)

$0 · Bring your own gear or rent ~$20

Wade out from the south end of Chill Island beach — there's a rocky reef area with colorful fish just 30–40 meters out. One parent snorkels while the other plays with the toddler in the shallows. Bring your own mask & snorkel for best value.

  • ✅ Free if you bring gear
  • ✅ Steps from the beach
  • ✅ Parrotfish, sergeant majors
  • ⚠️ Visibility varies by tide
🚤 Excursion

🐢 Guided Snorkel Tour

~$55–75/adult · ~45 min boat ride + snorkeling

Boat trip to a pristine reef with sea turtles, rays, and vibrant coral. Much better visibility and marine life than shore snorkeling. One parent goes while the other stays on the beach — then swap on the next departure!

  • ✅ Sea turtles & rays
  • ✅ Equipment included
  • ✅ Multiple departures — swap parents
  • ⚠️ ~2 hours total per parent

  • ✅ Drink package works!

7:00 – 8:00 AM

🍳 Breakfast + Prep

Full breakfast on board. Apply sunscreen, pack swim diapers, snacks & water. CocoCay tenders start early — line up by 7:45 to be first on the island and grab the best spots!

🚢 Get in Tender Line Early! 🍌 Pack Toddler Snacks
8:30 – 9:00 AM

🏝️ Arrive & Claim Your Spot

Tender to island (~10 min). Head straight to your chosen beach area — South Beach for free, or check in at Coco Beach Club. Grab chairs under shade before they fill up. The island is stroller-friendly on main paths!

🏃 Go Straight to Beach! 👶 Stroller-Friendly Paths
9:00 – 11:00 AM

🏖️ Beach + Snorkeling Time

Your toddler plays in the calm, knee-deep water (it stays shallow for 50+ feet!). Take turns snorkeling — one parent at a time. The water is warm, clear, and perfect. Build sandcastles, splash, collect shells! 🐚

👶 Ultra Shallow Water 🤿 Take-Turns Snorkel 🐚 Shell Collecting
11:00 AM – 12:00 PM

🍽️ Lunch Time

Hit the Chill Grill for free jerk chicken & BBQ, or enjoy the Beach Club buffet if you upgraded. The free food is genuinely good — don't overthink it! Your toddler will love the fruit and soft bread options.

🍗 Jerk Chicken 🦞 Beach Club Lobster 💵 Free options available
12:00 – 1:30 PM

😴 Toddler Nap + Explore

Stroller nap under a shaded palapa or head back to the ship for cabin nap (tenders run all day!). While toddler naps with one parent, the other can explore — try the Oasis Lagoon (largest freshwater pool in the Caribbean!) or grab a drink at the Floating Bar. 🍹

😴 Nap Window 🏊 Oasis Lagoon Pool 🍹 Floating Bar
1:30 – 3:00 PM

🌊 Afternoon Beach Session

After nap — back to the water! Afternoons are warmer and the island is less crowded as some guests return to the ship. Great time for more snorkeling, or just relaxing with your toes in the sand while your toddler splashes. Take family photos with the iconic blue water! 📸

🏖️ Less Crowded! 👶 More Splash Time 📸 Family Photos
3:00 – 3:30 PM

🛍️ Island Shopping

Browse the Straw Market for Bahamian crafts — straw bags, shell jewelry, handmade souvenirs. Pick up a little something as a CocoCay keepsake. The shops are shaded and air-conditioned — a nice break from the heat!

🛍️ Straw Market ❄️ Air-Conditioned!
3:30 – 4:30 PM

⛵ Tender Back to Ship

Last tender is typically around 4:30–5:00 PM — don't cut it close! Rinse off at the island showers, change your toddler, and enjoy the tender ride back. Watch the sunset from the ship deck tonight! 🌅

⚠️ Don't Miss Last Tender! 🌅 Sunset on Deck

💡 CocoCay Tips with a Toddler

▼
  • 🚤CocoCay is a tender port — you take a small boat from the ship. Strollers are allowed but fold yours before boarding the tender.
  • ⏰Get in line early (7:30 AM) — first tenders mean best beach spots and no crowds.
  • 💧Bring refillable water bottles — free water stations are spread across the island.
  • 🏊The Oasis Lagoon freshwater pool is massive and has a shallow end for toddlers.
  • 👶There are no changing facilities with baby tables — bring a portable changing pad in your bag.
  • 💵Everything is charged to your SeaPass card — no cash needed on the island.
  • 📶Ship WiFi doesn't work on the island — download offline entertainment for the toddler just in case.
  • 🧴Reef-safe sunscreen only! The Bahamas requires it to protect coral reefs.
